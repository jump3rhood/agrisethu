Our Purpose - A Better Future for Farmers

For many years, farming has been uncertain — changing wages, seasonal problems, and little recognition. At Agrisethu, we believe the people who feed the world deserve security, stability, and the utmost dignity.

You are not laborers, You are skilled professionals.
You are employees.

Why join us?

Here are the key benefits and work culture

Stable Employment

You get a regular monthly salary. No more tension of daily wages.

Professional Training

We train you in good farming practices, organic methods, and modern tools.

Safety & Compliance

We follow safe working conditions and make sure every farmer is protected.

Long-term Engagement

Regular work based on your skills in farming or animal husbandry.

Insurance

Insurance assistance for farmers, crop, land and livestock.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries from farmers and visitors

Q1: Will I get a monthly salary?

Yes. Every farmer working with Agrisethu receives a fixed monthly salary, just like any other employee.

Q2: Do I need my own land?

No. Land is provided based on the project. If you have land, that's also welcome, but it's not required.

Q3: Do I have to pay anything to join?

No. There is no joining fee, no hidden charges.

Q4: What work will I have to do?

Your regular farming work. We give guidance, tools, and support to make your work easier and more successful.

Q5: How do I join Agrisethu?

Just fill the form or click "Join as Farmer" on the website. Our team will contact you and guide you through the next steps.

Q6: Is this a government job?

No, it is not a government job. Agrisethu is a private organization, but we provide stable employment and support similar to a regular job. You can also contribute to Global markets.

Q7: Will I get training?

Yes. Every farmer gets free training so you can grow crops better and earn more.

Q8: What if I don't have experience?

No problem. We train beginners and guide them step by step.

Q9: What crops will I be working with?

It depends on the project and location. We mostly focus on vegetables, fruits, and high-demand crops.

Q10: Can women farmers join?

Absolutely. Women farmers are encouraged and welcomed.
