Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What makes Agrisethu's organic produce certified?

Our farms follow strict organic cultivation practices—no synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or GMOs. All produce undergoes third-party testing and meets international organic standards.

Q2: What is the minimum order quantity for exports?

We work with buyers of all sizes. For international shipments, we typically recommend starting with a half-container load (10-12 tons), but we can accommodate smaller trial orders depending on the product and destination.

Q3: How long does shipping take?

Delivery timelines depend on destination and shipping method. Air freight: 3-7 days. Sea freight: 15-30 days. We provide estimated delivery dates before you confirm orders.

Q4: Can I customize packaging and labeling?

Absolutely. We offer custom packaging, private labeling, and branding options to meet your market requirements. Additional costs may apply based on specifications.

Q5: How do you ensure consistent supply throughout the year?

Our network of managed farms across different regions allows us to rotate crops and maintain year-round availability for most products. We also plan cultivation based on advance orders.

Q6: What payment terms do you offer?

We work with flexible payment terms based on order size and partnership history. Options include advance payment, letter of credit (LC), and installment plans for established partners.

Q7: Do you handle all export documentation?

Yes. We manage phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, customs declarations, and all paperwork required for smooth clearance at your destination port.

Q8: What if there's a quality issue with my shipment?

Quality issues are rare due to our rigorous testing, but if any arise, we take full responsibility. We'll work with you to resolve the issue through replacement, credit, or appropriate compensation.

Q9: Can I visit the farms where my produce is grown?

Yes! We welcome buyer visits. Transparency is core to our values. Schedule a farm tour to see our operations firsthand and meet the farmers growing your produce.

Q10: How do I start a partnership with Agrisethu?

Simple. Contact our export team, share your requirements, and we'll send you our product catalog with specifications and pricing. Request samples, and once satisfied, we'll formalize the partnership agreement.
