Why Animal Husbandry...?

Not all land is ideal for cultivation. Soil type, water availability, or terrain might make crop farming challenging. That's where our animal husbandry solutions step in—turning limitations into opportunities with cattle, goats, poultry, and organic dairy operations.

Animal husbandry offers consistent revenue regardless of crop seasons, providing financial stability year-round.

Sustainable & Profitable

Types of Animal Husbandry

Dairy Cattle Farming

Produce high-quality milk and dairy products with proper cattle management. Learn about breeds, feeding, and milking techniques.

Poultry Farming

Raise chickens for eggs and meat production. Understand broiler and layer farming systems for maximum profitability.

Sheep Farming

Dual-purpose farming for wool and meat production. Discover suitable breeds and grazing management techniques.

Pig Farming

Efficient meat production with high feed conversion rates. Learn about housing, nutrition, and disease management.

Goat Farming

Versatile farming for milk, meat, and fiber. Perfect for small-scale operations with minimal space requirements.

Fish Farming (Aquaculture)

Cultivate fish in ponds or tanks. Explore freshwater and marine fish farming for sustainable protein production.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Will I get a monthly income?
Yes. Every livestock farmer working with AgriSethu receives regular income based on production and agreed terms, just like any other professional employment.

Q2: Do I need my own land?
No. Infrastructure is provided based on the project. If you have land, that's also welcome, but it's not required to get started.

Q3: Do I have to pay anything to join?
No. There is no joining fee, no hidden charges. We provide all necessary training and initial setup at no cost to you.

Q4: What animals will I be working with?
It depends on the project and location. We mostly focus on dairy cattle, poultry, goats, and sheep based on regional suitability and market demand.

Q5: How do I join AgriSethu?
Just fill the contact form or click "Join as Livestock Farmer" on the website. Our team will contact you and guide you through the next steps.

Q6: Will I get training?
Yes. Every farmer gets free professional training in modern livestock management, animal health, and best practices.

Q7: What if I don't have experience?
No problem. We train beginners and guide them step by step. Many of our successful farmers started with no prior livestock experience.

Q8: Is veterinary care provided?
Yes. We provide regular veterinary checkups, emergency care, and all necessary vaccinations for the animals under our management.

Q9: Can women farmers join?
Absolutely. Women farmers are encouraged and welcomed. We have many successful women livestock farmers in our network.

Q10: What about insurance?
We provide insurance coverage for livestock and provide assistance for farmers. Your investment and our animals are protected.
